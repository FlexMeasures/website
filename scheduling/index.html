Scheduling

The FlexMeasures platform optimizes schedules for your flexible assets. This is where energy flexibility is valorized!

Examples are:

  • Charging schedules of batteries
  • Heat pumps management
  • Buffering of machinery

The goals can be maximal cost savings, maximal usage of solar power or sable energy supply for the most crucial consumers.
